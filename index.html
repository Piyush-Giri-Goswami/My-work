Upcoming Tech Innovations

Hydrogen Train

The Hydrogen Train in India refers to a project that aims to introduce hydrogen-powered trains as an eco-friendly alternative

Generative AI

Generative AI, or generative artificial intelligence, is a type of AI that can create new content like text, images, videos, music, and audio

Robotic Process Automation (RPA)

Robotic process automation (RPA) is a software technology that uses robots to automate tasks by mimicking human actions

(BCI)Brain Computer Interface

Brain-Computer Interface (BCI) allows direct communication between the brain and external devices, enabling control of technology through thought alone. bypassing traditional methods of interaction like muscles or speech.

Eco Friendly Flights

Eco-friendly flights are flights that are made with the environment in mind. Eco-friendly flights aim to reduce environmental impact by using sustainable fuels, efficient aircraft, and optimized flight paths to lower emissions.

Space-Based Solar Power (SBSP)

Space-Based Solar Power (SBSP) is the concept of collecting solar energy in space and transmitting it to Earth, offering a constant, renewable energy source regardless of weather or daylight. To save electricity

Current Technology

Apple Vision Pro

Apple Vision Pro is designed to understand hand gestures from comfortable positions, like resting in your lap or on the sofa.

Delta Pro

DThe Delta Pro is a high-capacity portable power station by EcoFlow, designed for both home backup and outdoor use.

Magic Cooking Robot

Magic Cooking Robot is a versatile 7-in-1 kitchen robot that automates the cooking process with over 200 pre-loaded recipes.

Electic Vehicle

An electric vehicle (EV) is a vehicle that uses an electric motor to run on electricity stored in a battery instead of a fossil fuel like gasoline or diesel.

Autonomous Drones

Autonomous drones are unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs) that use artificial intelligence (AI) to operate independently without a human pilot, without human control.

Biotechnology In Agriculture

Agricultural biotechnology, also known as agritech, is the use of scientific tools and techniques to modify living organisms in agriculture, pest resistance.
